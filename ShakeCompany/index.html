About

Officially established in 2012 in the state of Goa in India, Shake-it-off began as a single-item food-truck which eventually evolved into a multi-drink shake company. From heavenly milkshakes, and now, stepping into the smoothies zone, we aim to bring you drinks that are delectable as well as an aesthetic delight.
